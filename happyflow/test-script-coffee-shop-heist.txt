COFFEE SHOP HEIST
A Short Film

Written for Koyal QA manual testing
Format: Plain text screenplay (.txt)

================================================================================
FADE IN:

EXT. DOWNTOWN STREET - MORNING

Rain hammers the pavement. Commuters rush under umbrellas. A yellow taxi splashes through a puddle.

A woman in a red coat, MAYA (32), checks her phone, then ducks into a coffee shop.

================================================================================

INT. BEAN & BREW COFFEE SHOP - CONTINUOUS

Warm light. Jazz plays low. The espresso machine hisses.

MAYA shakes rain from her coat and joins the line. Three customers ahead of her.

Behind the counter: LEO (28), barista, tired eyes, name tag slightly crooked.

CUSTOMER #1, an older man with a newspaper, steps forward.

CUSTOMER #1
Large black coffee. No room for nonsense today.

LEO
Coming right up, sir.

Leo pours. Maya glances at the clock on the wall: 8:47 AM.

CUSTOMER #2, a teenager with headphones, speaks without removing them.

CUSTOMER #2
Can I get a caramel thing? The big one. Extra caramel.

LEO
Venti caramel macchiato, extra caramel. Got it.

CUSTOMER #2
You're a legend.

Maya smiles despite herself. Her phone buzzes. She reads a text, jaw tightening.

INSERT - PHONE SCREEN

Message from "ALEX": "They moved the meet. 9:15. Same place. Don't be late."

BACK TO SCENE

CUSTOMER #3, a businesswoman, orders quickly.

CUSTOMER #3
Oat milk latte. Double shot. To go.

LEO
Name for the cup?

CUSTOMER #3
Sarah.

Maya steps up. For a moment, she and Leo lock eyes.

MAYA
Medium americano. And whatever pastry isn't sad.

LEO
That narrows it down to two. Almond croissant or blueberry muffin?

MAYA
Surprise me.

LEO
Risky before nine in the morning.

MAYA
I've had worse mornings.

Leo rings her up. The register beeps.

LEO
That'll be six-forty.

Maya taps her card. DECLINED.

MAYA
Try again.

Second tap. DECLINED.

MAYA (CONT'D)
(quietly)
You've got to be kidding me.

Leo hesitates, then lowers his voice.

LEO
Look, the muffin is on me. Just take it.

MAYA
I don't need charity.

LEO
Then call it professional courtesy. You look like you're about to fight someone.

MAYA
Maybe I am.

A door chime rings. A man enters: ALEX (35), leather jacket, scanning the room.

Alex spots Maya. His expression hardens.

ALEX
We need to talk. Outside. Now.

MAYA
I'm getting coffee.

ALEX
You can get coffee after you explain why our buyer thinks we're selling fake data.

Customers turn. Leo watches, uneasy.

MAYA
Keep your voice down.

ALEX
Then stop stalling. You said you had the drive at eight.

Maya pulls a small encrypted drive from her bag.

MAYA
I have it. But the decryption key isn't on here. It's in the safe deposit box.

ALEX
Then why are we in a coffee shop and not a bank?

MAYA
Because banks open at nine, Alex. And because you moved the meet twice.

Leo slides Maya's americano across the counter.

LEO
Medium americano. Almond croissant on the house.

Alex eyes Leo.

ALEX
Who's this?

MAYA
Nobody.

LEO
Actually, I'm Leo. I make coffee. I also hear everything, so maybe take the corporate espionage outside.

Alex steps closer to Leo.

ALEX
Mind your business.

LEO
Sir, this is a place of caffeine and mild jazz. Please step back.

Maya puts a hand on Alex's chest.

MAYA
Stop. He's right. We're drawing attention.

A barista in the back, PRIYA (26), calls out.

PRIYA
Leo, we need more oat milk up front!

Leo doesn't look away from Alex.

LEO
One second, Priya.

(to Alex)
Sir, I'm going to ask you once to lower your volume.

ALEX
Or what? You'll foam me to death?

MAYA
Alex. Enough.

Alex exhales, steps back. Maya grabs her coffee.

MAYA (CONT'D)
(to Leo)
Thank you. For the croissant.

LEO
Try the bank at nine. And maybe update your payment info.

Maya almost laughs.

MAYA
I'll add it to my list.

She heads for the door. Alex follows.

================================================================================

EXT. COFFEE SHOP - ALLEY BESIDE ENTRANCE - MOMENTS LATER

Rain lighter now. Maya and Alex stand under a narrow awning.

ALEX
The buyer lands at ten-thirty. If the key isn't real, we lose everything.

MAYA
The key is real. I copied it last night from the server room.

ALEX
You copied it alone?

MAYA
You were "busy" sending threatening texts.

ALEX
I was covering our tracks.

MAYA
You were covering your ego.

Alex rubs his temples.

ALEX
Fine. What do you need from me?

MAYA
Trust. And a car that doesn't smell like fast food.

ALEX
I can do one of those.

Maya sips her americano.

MAYA
Then drive. Bank opens in twelve minutes.

They hurry toward the street.

================================================================================

INT. FIRST NATIONAL BANK - LOBBY - LATER

Bright fluorescent lights. Security guard at the desk. Maya and Alex approach.

SECURITY GUARD
Good morning. How can I help you?

MAYA
Safe deposit access. Box 441. Maya Chen.

SECURITY GUARD
ID, please.

Maya hands over her ID. The guard checks it, nods.

SECURITY GUARD (CONT'D)
Sign here. Your associate will wait in the lobby.

ALEX
I'm not her associate. I'm her—

MAYA
Backup.

Alex crosses his arms. The guard buzzes them through.

================================================================================

INT. SAFE DEPOSIT VAULT - CONTINUOUS

Rows of metal boxes. Maya enters with a bank clerk, CLERK (40s).

CLERK
Box 441. Take your time.

The clerk steps away. Maya inserts her key, turns. Click.

Inside: an envelope labeled "KOYAL - PHASE 2".

Maya opens it. A single index card with handwritten numbers.

MAYA
(reading)
"Voice map seed: 7A-19-Falcon."

She photographs the card with her phone.

ALEX (O.S.)
(from doorway)
Did you get it?

MAYA
I got it. And if you follow me into the vault again without clearance, I'll leave you at the coffee shop.

ALEX
You'd miss me.

MAYA
I'd miss the croissant.

================================================================================

INT. BEAN & BREW COFFEE SHOP - AFTERNOON

The lunch rush. Maya returns alone, sits at a corner table, laptop open.

Leo delivers a fresh cup.

LEO
Second americano. You look like the bank went well.

MAYA
How do you know I went to a bank?

LEO
You said "twelve minutes" and left like someone in a heist movie.

MAYA
Maybe I am in a heist movie.

LEO
Then I'm the guy who gives the hero caffeine in act two.

Maya smiles. Genuine this time.

MAYA
Act two is when everything breaks.

LEO
Act two is when the hero fixes it.

Priya leans in from the counter.

PRIYA
Leo, table four wants decaf but "with energy."

LEO
That's just hope in a cup.

(to Maya)
Good luck with your... movie.

Leo walks off. Maya types on her laptop.

ON SCREEN - She uploads files to a folder named "Project - Coffee Shop Heist".

MAYA
(to herself)
Let's see if you can turn this into a film.

Her phone rings. ALEX.

MAYA (CONT'D)
(answering)
We have the key. Meet at the office.

ALEX (V.O.)
(filtered)
Buyer wants a preview scene by tonight. Dialogue-heavy. One location.

MAYA
Coffee shop?

ALEX (V.O.)
Coffee shop works.

Maya looks around Bean & Brew: customers, steam, rain on the window.

MAYA
Yeah. Coffee shop works.

She closes the laptop. The jazz track swells.

================================================================================

EXT. BEAN & BREW COFFEE SHOP - DUSK

Neon sign flickers on: "OPEN LATE."

Through the window, Maya and Leo talk at the counter. She gestures like she's directing a scene.

Leo nods, amused, and wipes the espresso machine.

FADE OUT.

================================================================================
THE END
